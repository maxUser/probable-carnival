Ear Syringing

If you're having difficulties hearing it may be due to a build up of cerumen (ear wax).
Other indications for Ear Syringing and cerumen removal include: earaches, hearing loss, ear fullness, or ear itchiness.
I will do a full assessment of the ear canal to determine if cerumen removal is appropriate.
For full effect, ear wax softeners prior to ear flushing may be required.
